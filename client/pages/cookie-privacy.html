Cookies & privacy policy

Last updated: june 30th 2026

Personal data we process

iidx.otoge.win processes your personal data because you use our services and/or because you provide it to us yourself. Below you will find an overview of the personal data that we process:

  • Your IIDX DJ ID
  • Your IIDX DJ Name
  • The scores you achieve on the score server
  • Other data you authorize the score server to share with us
iidx.otoge.win processes this data for the following purposes:
  • To provide you with the ability to register for the competition operated by the service
  • To deliver the competition as a service
  • To deliver the historic record of the competition as a service

Our website and/or service does not intend to collect data about website visitors who are under 16 years old. Unless they have permission from their parents or guardians. We cannot however verify if a visitor is over 16 years old. We therefore recommend that parents also be involved in the online activities of their children, to prevent the collection of data about minors without parental consent. If you are convinced that we have collected personal data about a minor without that consent, please contact @boonsboos on Discord, and we will delete this information.

How long we retain personal data

iidx.otoge.win retains your personal data only as long as strictly necessary to achieve the purposes for which your data is collected. Your scores are retained for archiving purposes and to display the score history. Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your account data will be deleted. If you want your score attack profile to be deleted, please contact @boonsboos on Discord.

Sharing of personal data with third parties

iidx.otoge.win shares your personal data with third parties only if this is necessary for the execution of our agreement with you or to comply with a legal obligation.

Cookies or similar techniques used by the service

iidx.otoge.win does not make use of cookies or similar techniques that store data on the device you use to visit the site.

Viewing, modifying, or deleting your data

You have the right to view your personal data, correct it, or delete it. Additionally, you have the right to withdraw your consent for data processing or object to the processing of your personal data by iidx.otoge.win. Additionally, you have the right to data portability. That means that you can submit a request to us to send the personal data that we have about you in a computer file to you or another organization. You can send a request for access, correction, deletion, data transfer of your personal data or a request to withdraw your consent or object to the processing of your personal data to @boonsboos on Discord. iidx.otoge.win also wants to point out that you have the option to file a complaint with the national supervisory authority, the Dutch Data Protection Authority. You can do this via the following link: Submitting a tip-off or a complaint to the AP

How we protect your data

iidx.otoge.win takes the protection of your data seriously and takes appropriate measures to prevent misuse, loss, unauthorized access, unwanted disclosure, and unauthorized modification. If you feel that your data is not properly secured or there are indications of abuse, please contact @boonsboos on Discord as soon as possible.
